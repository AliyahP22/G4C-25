Do you want to lend a helping hand? In GreenQuest, you take on the role of a character of your choice navigating the community around you, taking on simple tasks like cleaning a park or planting more trees to help the community become unified. Use problem solving skills ,stealth, and hints around you to take on the interactive battle of saving the community! Will you do your part and help make the community a better and greener environment, or will you get stuck with the tricky animals scattering litter around? Make choices wisely, every choice matters.

In GreenQuest, the theme of nurturing your nature and preserving the environment is reflected in the scenery of a green park with clear ponds and a blue sky, surrounded by not only pretty plants, but also trash destroying the environment.. Players learn about this theme through simple yet complex tasks, where each command and action done has a consequence which could help nurture the environment or make it more polluted. Completing tasks such as running around and picking up trash, and planting more flowers reinforces the theme of conserving the environment and nurturing your nature, making the community happier. As the user progresses through the game, they would be able to help the polluted environment become a greener, eco-friendly environment, where there are more plants and trees in a clean environment.

Growing up, I have always been fascinated by nature and how beautiful it can be. I was very fond of the different flowers during spring time and how each flower was different and yet so beautiful. My family and I would always plant during the spring and summer, whether it'd be simple roses, or pepper plants. Nature was always an aspect that made me happy and in the world today, pollution and global warming are common problems surrounding nature. I was inspired to make a game that is fun, interactive, yet informative on how we can play our part in keeping the environment eco-friendly. GreenQuest is a game where players can do simple tasks ans learn how they can help the environment, and can understand the lasting effects of happiness nature has on us.
